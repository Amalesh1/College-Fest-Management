About Croeso

Croeso is our college ABC's annual Cultural Fest. The union members of the college organize this event every year in the month of April. It started 5 years ago and since then it's keep going. Before the event we ask for permission from our principle and local police station to conduct the event peacefully. We invite neighbour colleges and vendors. The invited companies make the event more attractive. All the students enjoy music, technical and cultural events, food, dj etc. Vendors also earn some profit. During the fest students gets opportunity to learn new things. They experiances memorable things in this event. Every year we are adding many new events so that students can enjoy the fest more.
